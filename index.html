About us:

As the organizers of Beirut Marathon, we take great pride in hosting this annual running event since 2003. Our goal has always been to promote healthy living and foster a sense of community in Lebanon. We believe that the Beirut Marathon has become a symbol of unity and hope for our country, as participants from all over the world join us to run for a cause and support our community. We are committed to organizing a world-class event that showcases the beauty of Beirut and inspires people to live healthier and more active lives.
